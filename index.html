Have questions about working with Grubhub? We have answers.

  • What is the Grubhub for Restaurants portal?

    When you sign up for Grubhub Marketplace, you get access to Grubhub for Restaurants, which is a self-service portal that lets you manage your Grubhub account and business. You can do the following from the Grubhub for Restaurants portal:

    • Manage the ordering process.
    • Update your menu as it appears live on the Grubhub site.
    • Upload logos and menu photos.
    • Schedule a free photography session.
    • Review financials.
    • Review order history.
    • Create promotions.
    • Receive data-driven insights on your business performance compared to local restaurants.
  • What tools does Grubhub provide partners?

    The Grubhub for Restaurants portal or merchant portal provides tools to manage every part of your Grubhub account, including ordering, promotions, menus and restaurant details like hours. In addition to these tools, every Grubhub partner gets a dedicated Account Advisor invested in the success of your restaurant. Grubhub also offers technology and point-of-sale integrations for partners.
